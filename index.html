My Roadmap

2016

O levels

After a long school life at Mastermind School, I was done with my O levels (Edexcel IGCSE) in 2016. I took a total of 10 subjects including general subjects and pure science subjects along with Economics, Accounting and ICT. I achieved a Daily Star Award and an Edexcel Award for my academic achievements. I scored seven A*s, two As and one B grades.

2017

Graphic Designing and Web Development Courses

In 2017, during my first year of high school, I started learning multidisciplinary extracurricular activities like art and french. The most major of them were graphic designing and web development. I soon lost interest in web development then and moved completely to artistic ventures. I headed the graphics team for all of the major events organized by our high school clubs in 2017-18 which was my first major professional experience in working with a committed team.

2018

A Levels

A-levels exams were knocking at the door in mid 2018. By the end of 2018, I was done with my A-levels (Edexcel IALs) as well. I took a total of five subjects - Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics and Further Mathematics. I achieved a Daily Star Award for my academic achievements and graduated from High school with honors. I scored one A* in Mathematics, and four B grades. It was time to apply for colleges.

2019

Animation Lab, DU

I could not get into various elite universities due to financial reasons and I also lost a number of scholarships in 2019. I decided to learn 3D animation while waiting for an opportunity for college admissions. I joined a programme (Animation Lab, CSE, DU) organized by the CSE department of the University of Dhaka and learnt all I could in the 1.5 year course. It was truly an unforgettable experience which formed a base to what I am doing right now. I graduated there with a 3.23 CGPA in June 2020 during COVID-19 crisis.

2020

North South University
BSCSE

Unable to find any other options, I joined North South University in the BSCSE programme to major in Computer Science and Engineering. I am currently a student who stumbled upon a newfound interest in programming and development. This website may stand as proof of that I believe. I now have a dream to design and create my own games and possibly establish a game development company in the future. I still have two/three years of college left though.

2021

Poison Studios - Founder

In February 2021, I started Poison Studios with a classmate of mine from Animation Lab, CSE, DU. We focused mainly on creating stylized assets for games and animated films/ads. We started taking work from freelance platforms like Fiverr and Upwork. By February 2022, we stood over a well-oiled machine of a venture with a base of clients looking for quality work. We decided to try expanding, so lets see where we go.
